We are here to help

Tocado’s Law Firm provides trusted, personalized legal support for individuals and families. We fight for your rights with integrity, precision, and a client-first approach — whether you’re facing immigration, civil, or family law challenges.

01
Immigration Law
Effective legal representation begins with strategic, compassionate immigration representation in complex matters—deportation defense, bond hearings, USCIS interviews, family petitions, humanitarian relief, and more. From unaccompanied minors to crime victims and detained individuals, we fight for your future with experience in court, USCIS, and consular proceedings. Justice knows no borders—neither do we.
02
Dependency Cases
We protect the rights of vulnerable children and families in sensitive dependency matters, including immigrant minors seeking SIJS. Our firm guides clients through DCF investigations, court hearings, and collaborates closely with immigration counsel. Compassionate, strategic, and child-centered advocacy—because every young life deserves stability, protection, and a path forward.
03
Criminal Defense & 'Crim-migration'
Strategic advocacy makes the difference when defending clients facing criminal charges—from DUI and theft to domestic violence—while safeguarding their immigration future. Our integrated crimmigration approach evaluates legal consequences and builds unified defense strategies across criminal and immigration courts. Strategic, bilingual, and tenacious—we protect your record, your rights, and your ability to remain in the U.S.
04
Civil Litigation
We represent individuals and businesses in high-stakes civil disputes, including personal injury, contract breaches, fraud, and non-payment claims. Whether negotiating settlements or litigating in court, we pursue justice with precision and persistence. Our goal: real results, fair compensation, and strategic solutions that protect your rights and restore what’s owed.
04
Family Law
Clients turn to Tocado’s Law Firm for guidance through life’s most personal legal challenges—divorce, custody, support, and protective orders—with clarity, compassion, and fierce advocacy. Whether contested or amicable, we protect your rights and prioritize your family’s well-being. Thoughtful counsel and strategic representation when it matters most—because family deserves both strength and understanding.
